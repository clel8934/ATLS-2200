The Coffee Culture

One of the world's most popular drinks, coffee dates back to the 9th century. It is said that Kaldi, a young goatherder from Ethiopia, tried the local berries and brought them home. Despite, coffee beans are actually seeds - specifically, 'coffee bean' is the pit of a coffee cherry. Although coffee comes from cherries, not grapes, the word originated from 'wine' - the Arabic term "qahwah". The Dutch then named it "koffie" followed by the English name that was derived in 15

Coffee Consumption

Approximately 10 million tonnes of coffee are produced every year. People claim that coffee is the second most traded commodity in the world. The tallest cup of coffee ever drank was over 11 feet tall - more than 18,000 liters of coffee! The most expensive coffee in the world is Kopi Luwak; the coffee is from Indonesia and can cost over $500 per kilo. Coffee can help you live longer and Finnish people consume the most coffee.
